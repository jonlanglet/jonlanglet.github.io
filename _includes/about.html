## About me

First year PhD student at Queen Mary University of London, under the supervision of Dr. Gianni Antichi.
I am a part of the Networks group, with a research focus on programmable network data planes.

I earned my Bachelor's and Master's degrees at Karlstad University in Sweden year 2019 and 2020 respectively. During this time, I also worked part-time as a lab supervisor and research assistant at the university.

My research interests include data plane programmability, network measurements, computational offloading, real-time systems, end host networking, and machine learning.
